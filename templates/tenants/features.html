📒 Accounting

A real double-entry general ledger — not a glorified spreadsheet. Every transaction is balanced, immutable, and auditable.

Double-entry journal

Every business event posts a balanced DR/CR transaction through a single posting service. No way to create unbalanced entries.

Immutable history

Voiding an invoice, bill, or journal generates a reversing entry instead of mutating the original. Historical records never change.

Chart of Accounts

Pre-seeded with a sensible default CoA on tenant creation. Add, rename, and reorganize accounts as needed.

Payments & applications

Record customer payments against invoices and vendor payments against bills with partial-allocation support.

Full audit trail

Every record carries a history via django-simple-history — who changed what, when, and why.

Source document linking

Posted journal entries link directly back to the originating invoice, bill, MO, or stock movement.

📦 Inventory Management

Open source inventory management with proper costing — not just quantity tracking.

Weighted average costing

Every receipt automatically recalculates the moving average cost: ((qty × cost) + (recv × recv_cost)) / (qty + recv).

Stock movements ledger

Every quantity change is a Receipt, Issue, or Adjustment row — a complete history of every unit that ever moved.

Low-stock alerts

Set thresholds per product. Items below threshold show red warning badges in the catalog and on the dashboard.

Product images

Upload JPG, PNG, GIF, or WebP up to 5 MB. Automatic SKU-letter fallback avatars when no image is uploaded.

Book vs physical reconciliation

Dashboard widget matches GL inventory value against physical stock value to surface variances instantly.

Stock-on-hand views

Live quantity views per product with location-ready architecture for future multi-warehouse expansion.

🛒 Sales & Order-to-Cash

The full O2C cycle from quote to cash, with automatic GL postings and COGS recognition.

Customer profiles

Contact info, billing & shipping addresses, lifetime revenue, outstanding AR, and full SO/invoice history.

Sales orders

Multi-line orders with quantity, unit price, and per-line GL account. Confirm to issue stock and draft an invoice in one click.

Customer invoices

Draft → Sent → Paid workflow with automatic COGS and inventory postings on send. Void posts a reversing entry.

Atomic confirm

If stock is insufficient, the entire confirmation rolls back. No partial state, no orphaned movements.

Accounts receivable

Open AR balance per customer, payment application, and aging visibility through the GL.

Branded PDFs PDF

One-click ReportLab PDF generation for sales orders and invoices with consistent corporate styling.

🧾 Purchasing & Procure-to-Pay

PO → Receipt → Bill — a complete P2P loop with automatic inventory and GL updates.

Vendor profiles

Contact info, default expense account, lifetime spend, outstanding AP, and full PO/bill history.

Purchase orders

Multi-line POs with quantity, unit cost, and expected delivery. Approve and track status to receipt and billing.

Goods receipts

Receive partial or full quantities. Each receipt posts inventory movements at the received unit cost.

Auto-generated bills

Generate a draft vendor bill directly from a goods receipt — lines pre-populated, duplicate-bill protection built in.

Vendor bills

Bills post AP and expense (or inventory) accounts. Void posts a reversing entry. AP aging visible through GL.

Branded PDFs PDF

One-click PDF generation for purchase orders.

🏭 Manufacturing

Open source manufacturing software with bills of materials, cost rollups, and production tracking.

Bills of materials

Multi-component BOMs with quantity and unit-of-measure per component. Easy to clone and version.

Dynamic cost rollups

Finished-good standard cost computed live from current component costs. Always reflects real-time material pricing.

Manufacturing orders

Create production orders against a BOM with target quantity. System computes total component needs.

Shortage visualizer

Order detail page shows green/red badges for each component — instantly see what's available vs short.

Atomic completion

Completing an MO atomically issues raw materials, receives finished goods, and posts the GL transaction.

Balanced GL postings

Every production run posts balanced DR Finished Goods / CR Raw Materials inventory journal entries.

📊 Reports & Analytics

Real-time financial reports plus an interactive dashboard for ops visibility.

Trial Balance

All accounts with DR/CR totals at any date. Click any account to drill into the GL.

Profit & Loss

Revenue, COGS, gross profit, expenses, and net income for any date range.

Balance Sheet

Assets, liabilities, and equity at any point in time. Balances every time, by design.

General Ledger

Full transactional GL with drill-downs to source documents and historical edit trail.

Live KPIs

YTD revenue, cost base, net profit, AR/AP outstanding — refreshed every page load.

Chart.js dashboards

Monthly cash flow line charts and inventory value doughnut charts with top-N aggregation.

🔒 Platform & Security

Multi-tenant architecture with real isolation, modern auth, and abuse protection.

Schema-per-tenant

Each workspace is a separate PostgreSQL schema via django-tenants. Physical isolation, not just row-level.

Self-serve signup

Email-verified workspace provisioning. Pick a subdomain, confirm via email, get a private ERP in seconds.

Argon2 passwords

Modern, memory-hard password hashing — the current best-practice algorithm.

HTTPS & HSTS

Enforced TLS in production with 1-year HSTS including all subdomains. CSRF protection on every form.

Rate limiting

Signup attempts capped per IP. Login attempts capped per account. Write rate-limited per user inside each workspace.

CAPTCHA on signup

Cloudflare Turnstile blocks bot signups while staying invisible to humans.

⬇️ Data, Documents & UI

Everything exportable, every record linked, all in a modern personalized UI.

CSV ZIP exports

One-click export of any combination of tables as Excel-ready CSV files in a ZIP archive.

JSON backups

Full database snapshots in standard Django fixture format. Restore with manage.py loaddata.

CSV bulk import

Map columns to fields with create-or-update logic and full transactional rollback on any validation error.

Drag-and-drop dashboard

Rearrange app tiles on the homepage. Layout persists per-user in browser localStorage.

Star & pin shortcuts

Star any module to pin it as a quick-access badge in the global navbar.

Deep linking everywhere

Every document reference in the UI is a clickable link — customers, vendors, SKUs, invoices, journals, MOs, everything.
